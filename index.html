Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions

What is the role of a Company Secretary in India?

▼

A Company Secretary in India serves as a key managerial personnel responsible for ensuring compliance with various corporate laws, maintaining statutory records, facilitating board meetings, and acting as a liaison between the company and regulatory authorities like MCA, SEBI, and ROC.

How can MSA Professional help my business?

▼

MSA Professional offers a comprehensive range of company secretary services including company formation, statutory compliance, corporate governance, and specialized advisory for IPOs and foreign investments. Our expert team ensures that your business remains compliant with all regulatory requirements while providing strategic guidance to enhance operational efficiency.

Is it mandatory for my company to appoint a Company Secretary?

▼

Under the Companies Act, 2013, every listed company and certain classes of unlisted companies must appoint a qualified Company Secretary as a Key Managerial Personnel.
